Patrick's Burgers

Description

Everyones had a burger before. Some good, some alright, and some burnt to a crisp. But have you ever had a burger so good, that you had to know its secrets? Well today you are going to learn how to cook the best burger ever.

Ingredients

  • Potato Buns
  • 2lbs 73/27 burger meat
  • White Onion
  • American Cheese Slices
  • Dill Pickle Chips
  • Condiments of your choice
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Heat medium sauce pan on high.
  2. Mix burger meat with 2 eggs and seasonings of your choice," (I recommend garlic powder, salt, and pepper)."
  3. Form meat into medium size balls and smash them onto the pan
  4. Throw minced onions in the pan while burgers cook.
  5. Place cheese onto burgers as they cook.
  6. Place burgers oon garnished buns as they ready.
